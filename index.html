ACRA Data Breach

The Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA) has suffered one of the most disastrous cybersecurity failures in Singapore’s history, leading to an increase in scams and financial losses amounting to millions. The exposure of NRIC numbers has enabled criminal syndicates to profile, target, and exploit victims with alarming precision.

Despite this massive failure, no PAP minister has been held accountable. Meanwhile, opposition leader Pritam Singh was convicted in a biased process led by a committee dominated by PAP ministers who voted 5-1 against him, showing how the government shields its own members while attacking the opposition.
